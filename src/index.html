Atomex hybrid exchange

Combines advantages of centralized and decentralized exchanges

  • You're in control

    Private keys are kept encrypted on your computer. Cross-chain transactions are carried out using atomic swaps.

  • No additional fees

    You pay only miner commission for both regular and cross-chain transactions.

  • Stay incognito

    No registration or identity verification required. However, all addresses are checked against abuses and suspicious origin of funds.

  • Liquidity provider

    Your counterparty is not an ordinary user, but a professional market maker whose task is to ensure sufficient liquidity.

  • Trading APIs

    Atomex provides websocket API for wallets and market makers. Contact us if you have questions regarding integration.

  • Passive income

    Staking Tezos brings passive income automatically. Decentralized Lending features coming soon. You can check delegation instruction.
